LUNCH OF THE DAY

Orange Chicken with Jasmine Rice

Buy orange chicken package from Walmart and 90 second ready rice. Then put the orange chicken in an air fryer for 10 minutes at 400 degrees. Heat up the packet of sauce. When the air fryer is done take the orange chicken out and add sauce. Heat the rice in microwave for 90 seconds. Serve and enjoy!

Add red crushed peppers for extra spice!

Chik Fil A Nuggets

Buy chik fila package from Walmart and Chikfila sauce. Then put the nuggets in an air fryer for 10 minutes at 350 degrees. When the air fryer is done take the nuggets and add sauce. Serve and enjoy!

Chikfila sauce can be purchased at most grocery stores, or a substitute sauce can be used.
